Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Home? ▼
AI Home is a curated directory of 60+ AI tools organized by category. It's completely free to browse and helps you find the right AI tool for any task — from chatbots and image generation to coding assistants and productivity apps.
Is AI Home free to use? ▼
Yes! AI Home is completely free to browse. The tools listed each have their own pricing (Free, Freemium, or Paid) which is shown on every tool card. Our built-in AI chatbot is also free to use.
How does the AI chatbot work? ▼
Our built-in AI chatbot is powered by Anthropic's Claude AI. It knows about all 60+ tools in our directory and can recommend the right tools for your needs, compare options, answer questions about AI, and help you get started. Your conversations are private and not stored.
Which AI chatbot is best for students? ▼
For students, we recommend starting with ChatGPT (free tier), Claude (free tier), or Google Gemini (free). All three are excellent for writing essays, understanding concepts, solving problems, and doing research. DeepSeek and HuggingChat are also completely free alternatives worth trying.
What's the difference between ChatGPT and Claude? ▼
ChatGPT (by OpenAI) and Claude (by Anthropic) are both excellent AI assistants. ChatGPT is known for its wide range of plugins, image generation via DALL·E, and broad knowledge. Claude excels at reading long documents, nuanced writing, coding, and following complex instructions carefully. Both are highly recommended — try both and see which feels more natural to you!
How do I get the best results from AI tools? ▼
The key is being specific in your prompts. Instead of "write an essay," try "write a 500-word essay about climate change for a high school audience, with an introduction, 3 body paragraphs, and conclusion." The more detail you give, the better the output. You can also ask AI to revise, improve, or explain its reasoning.

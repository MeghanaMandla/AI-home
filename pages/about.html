The story behind this project — built by a student, for everyone who wants to explore the world of AI.

Hi! I'm Meghana — a student who's passionate about AI, technology, and making the web more useful. I built AI Home because I kept finding myself searching for "which AI tool is best for X?" — so I decided to build one place that answers that question for everyone.

I know Python and HTML/CSS, and this project has been a fun way to combine my love of design with my interest in AI tools. I hope AI Home helps you discover something amazing!

🎯 The Mission

AI tools are growing at an incredible pace — there are thousands of them now, and new ones launch every week. The problem? Most people don't know which ones exist, which are free, or which one is right for their specific need.

AI Home solves that by being a clean, fast, and organized directory — with a built-in AI chatbot that can help guide you to the right tool.
